About JJ Foundation

We Are Non-Profit Organization

JJ Foundation, based in Ahmedabad, is a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting the lives of the underprivileged, under-supported, and socially neglected individuals of society. With a strong commitment to community welfare, the foundation operates with the core values of compassion, service, and inclusivity.
