About JJ Foundation

Empowering Lives. Building Hope. Creating Change.

Founded and based in Ahmedabad, JJ Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to uplifting the lives of the underprivileged, under-supported, and socially neglected segments of society. We work tirelessly to bring hope, dignity, and opportunity to individuals and families who have long been marginalized.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive society where every individual—regardless of their background—has equal access to basic human rights, opportunities for growth, and a life of dignity.

Our Mission

To serve vulnerable communities by providing sustainable support through education, healthcare, livelihood training, women empowerment, and social inclusion initiatives.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We serve with a deep sense of empathy and kindness.

Service

We are dedicated to selfless action that creates meaningful impact.

Inclusivity

We embrace diversity and advocate for equality and participation for all.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct.

Empowerment

We believe in enabling individuals to take control of their own futures.

Self-help groups and legal aid

Our Impact

Since its inception, JJ Foundation has positively touched thousands of lives through compassionate intervention and community-driven initiatives. From slum rehabilitation and school supplies distribution to skill-building workshops and mental health awareness drives, our work continues to evolve to meet the needs of society's most vulnerable.
